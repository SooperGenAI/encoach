The internship is not an add-on to the Encoach.ai program — it is the point of it. Every program is designed to make the internship work, and every internship is designed to produce demonstrated operational readiness, not a participation certificate.

## How it is structured

Encoach.ai trainees enter the internship having already developed working fluency in AI-enabled accounting administration, workflow-driven operations, and the Memintel-Canvas platform. The 2-month placement inside a corporate finance function builds on that foundation with:

- **Defined operational deliverables**, scoped by the host function
- **Supervisory structure** with clear review cadences and feedback loops
- **Workflow assignments** that mirror the work full-time team members do
- **End-of-placement evaluation** capturing what the trainee delivered, supervised, and certified

## What corporates get

A working operator — one who arrives ready to operate inside an AI-enabled workflow environment from day one, and who can take on real, supervised, accountable work for the duration of the placement.

## What trainees get

A documented operational track record at the start of their career — not exposure, not theory, but a demonstration of readiness inside a real finance function.
