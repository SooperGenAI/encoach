Memintel-Canvas is the operational environment Encoach.ai students train on. It is not a learning sandbox — it is the same class of platform that defines modern AI-enabled finance administration.

## What it is

Memintel-Canvas brings together four capabilities that, in combination, define what AI-enabled accounting administration looks like:

- **AI systems** — purpose-built for accounting administration, not retrofitted general-purpose models
- **Workflow intelligence** — end-to-end orchestration of finance operations, with supervisory checkpoints built in
- **Operational supervision** — humans certify, AI executes; the supervisory model is part of the design
- **Experts in the loop** — domain experts are a designed feature of the platform, not a service tier

## Why we train on it

Because the operating pattern of the platform is the operating pattern of the future finance function. Trainees who become fluent on Memintel-Canvas develop the operational instincts that transfer directly to working inside any modern AI-enabled finance environment.
